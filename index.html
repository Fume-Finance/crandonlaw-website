— Story

A boutique built on purpose.

Crandon Law was founded in 2010 in San Francisco with a single hypothesis: that a small group of senior lawyers, working virtually, could deliver the most complex fund formation work in the country with the speed and intimacy of a true boutique. Fifteen years later, the firm has formed close to eighty vehicles and counts clients who have moved with us from Fund I through Fund V.

We work with fund principals — particularly spin‑outs from leading funds and managers launching their first or second institutional vehicle — who want a senior, deeply technical partner without the structural overhead of a large firm. Our clients tend to have done this before, and our practice is built around that level of sophistication.
